Shaurma

A simple and quick recipe for delicious shaurma.

Shaurma is a very tasty dish. And cooked at home, it is also useful.

Ingredients

  • Chicken fillet
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Cabbage
  • Mayo
  • Lavash

Steps

  1. Vegetables - cucumber and tomato wash well.
  2. Cut the chicken fillet into thin strips and fry in a hot frying pan with the addition of a small amount of vegetable oil.
  3. Shred the cabbage finely.
  4. Cucumber and tomato cut into thin strips.
  5. Spread a sheet of pita bread on the table and brush it with the prepared mayonnaise sauce.
  6. Put the fried meat, cucumbers and tomatoes.
  7. Fry the finished shawarma in a dry frying pan or on the grill on both sides.
