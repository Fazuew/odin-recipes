Pilaf

Pilaf, which always turns out.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Carrot
  • Bulb onions
  • Garlic
  • Hot pepper
  • Zira and other spices - to taste
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into pieces.
  2. We cut the onion into cubes, carrots - into fairly large cubes.
  3. In a preheated cauldron, lightly fry the onion in vegetable oil.
  4. Add meat, fry until almost done.
  5. We fall asleep carrots, fry. Salt, add spices.
  6. We wash the rice well, put it in a cauldron, mix and pour boiling water so that the water covers the rice by 2-2.5 cm.
  7. Bring to a boil without covering with a lid.
  8. As soon as it boils, add whole heads of garlic, peeled from the top husk, and hot pepper pods. Reduce the heat to a minimum, cover with a lid and leave the pilaf for 20 minutes.
  9. Turn off the fire and let the pilaf stand for 15-20 minutes.
